My Journey

So far..

Working most of my 20's in the hospitality industry was an experience to say the least, I managed to learn how hard work can really pay off if you put your mind into it. After finally achieving my goal in 2018, from being a Busser to excelling into becoming the restaurant Manager, it was something I am really proud of achieving. Unfortunately, after only a year of being on the post, the restaurant that I have committed to and put my hard work into closed down.


Why i choose to study?...

Choosing to go back and study might sound crazy for some, especially after working hard to lay a career path in one industry, and now having to do it all over again will definitely be a challenge, and without trying to sound too cheesy “it’s a challenge I’m looking forward to overcome.” The reason why i choose to study is because I believe that learning the fundamentals is essential and will provide me with the tools i need to build a strong foundation in my future career as a Software developer.


What I am Currently doing?...

Currently I am in my first year of Level 3 Computer Science course, having achieve excellent grade in the previous year, and set to do the same this year. I am enjoying the different aspect of Computer Science and the potential benefits it will help me improve my skills and what i can bring to the table.

Units that have caught my interest are:

  • Website Development
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • The Principles of Computer Science
  • System Security and Encryption.

On top of the learning the core units in College, I also try and learn as much as can outside of it, taking Udemy courses like "Automate the Boring stuff with Python" and "Python Mega Course". The reason why want to learn Python as my first High-Level programming language is because, it’s a lot easier to understand compared to other languages, its used by companies that i enjoy using and i enjoyed watching Monty python as kid.
